MAKING AFRICA A BETTER PLACE FOR ALL OF US

Our Story

KULENGA AFRICA is a mother creative institution that was founded to inspire creativity in Africa. It believes in the creative ability of the mind to advance and amplify the course of creation. It is a place where a group of people that had been held back by un-civilization, ignorance and darkness are set free to express their creative abilities. ItIt KULENGA SCIENCE INITIATIVE is the bedrock of the African scientific state of mind, providing a platform to properly discover, harness and efficiently direct all the African creative energy for the growth of Africa and helping reducing social inequity. The word "kulenga" is a chewa word for being creative.

Our Vision

To inspire creativity and create the platform for scientific discoveries from Africa.

Our Mission

To reduce the social inequity in Africa using a scientific approach by informal public advocacy programs and launching scientific and community projects.

Our values

We believe in the supremacy of God as the owner and controller of the universe. Our scientific pivot is based on rational creationism. We believe in the potential ability of all men, regardless of race or color. To express scientific and inventive creative abilities. We believe in unity, peace and extensive collaborations. We believe in strength hidden in diversity We believe in excellence and zero-tolerance to mediocrity.
